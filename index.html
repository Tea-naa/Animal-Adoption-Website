About Happy Paws

At Happy Paws Pet Adoption Agency, our mission is to create loving and lasting connections between pets and families. Since 2020, we have rescued, rehabilitated, and rehomed animals in need, ensuring they receive the care and attention they deserve. Our dedicated team of volunteers and staff works tirelessly to match each pet with the perfect forever home through a comprehensive adoption process.
